
# Micro‑Liturgies for Projection / Text Inserts

Short phrases to flicker in visuals, titles, or liners.

## Movement 1 – the angels who learned to hover
- wings without feathers  
- the hum remembers you  

## Movement 2 – impact studies for a fallen choir
- impact is a language  
- angels don’t brake  

## Movement 3 – chorus of the un‑gentle
- bodies made of signal  
- not gentle, never gentle  

## Movement 4 – litany for what still rises
- gravity is a suggestion  
- the sky keeps calling  

## Global
- blessed are the unblinking  
- hallelujah in hertz  
- the drones inherit the ash  
- this is our upward noise
